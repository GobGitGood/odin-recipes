Pineapple Burgers

Description

I love pineapples and I love burgers so here is my recipe for pineapple burgers!!!

Ingredients

  • pineapple
  • ground beef
  • mayonnaise
  • teryaki sauce
  • hamburger buns
  • honey
  • cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Grill the burgers and pineapple slices.
  2. Mix other ingredients together for sauce.
  3. Enjoy
